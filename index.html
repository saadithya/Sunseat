App Permissions

SunSeat requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Location (Fine & Coarse): Used to determine your current position for route planning and sun position calculations at your location.
  • Internet Access: Required to fetch route data and geocoding information from OpenRouteService.

Your location data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or shared with third parties.
