Introduction

Scholarship ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the SunSeat mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Location Information

SunSeat collects your device's location data (GPS coordinates) to:

  • Determine your current position as a starting point for route planning
  • Calculate accurate sun position relative to your location
  • Provide route analysis for your journey

Important: Location data is processed locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or share your location data with any third parties.

Route Information

When you search for directions, the App sends your origin and destination to OpenRouteService API to retrieve route data. This information is used solely for providing navigation and sun exposure analysis.

How We Use Your Information

All data processing occurs locally on your device. We use the information solely to:

  • Calculate sun position and exposure during your journey
  • Provide route recommendations
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

SunSeat does not store any personal data on external servers. All calculations and data processing happen locally on your device. We do not use cloud storage for your location or route data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenRouteService: For geocoding and route directions. Data sent includes location coordinates only.

Children's Privacy

SunSeat does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
